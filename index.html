Cherry blossom's full-bloom date and temperature

Japan Meteorological Agency releases monthly temperature data since 1880 in Kyoto, Japan.

Many meteorologists say cherry blossoms' first-blooming and full-blooming are closely related to temperature in the previous winter and early spring.

I downloaded monthly average temperature data from November to March since 1880, as well as average temperature every 10 days during that time (early November, mid-November, late November, ... to late March).
